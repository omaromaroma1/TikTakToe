How to Play

  • 4x4 Grid: The board is larger than traditional tic tac toe
  • 3 in a Row: Get 3 of your marks in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) to win
  • Power Moves: Every 3 moves, the current player can remove one opponent's piece for free!
  • Turn-based: Players alternate between X and O
  • Strategy: Use power moves wisely to block your opponent's winning combinations

Rules Summary

  • Click any empty cell to place your mark
  • Watch the counter in the game info - it tells you when your power move is ready
  • Click "Remove Opponent's Piece" when you have a power move available
  • First to get 3 in a row wins!
